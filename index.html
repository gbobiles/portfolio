About Me What I love My Experience

Hey there!

First of all, thanks for visiting my portfolio! You can call me Gerald, 27 year old and married (hooray!). Creating and designing websites is my passion, I am always fascinated by the facts that everything we use online on our daily lives was build by strings of letters, characters and numbers.

I love creating and designing websites!

For me, it is something that not everyone can do but once you created a website, it'll be something that everyone can find useful. For me this is the greatest revolutionary invention next to the wheel and electricity.
