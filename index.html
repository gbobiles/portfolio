About Me What I love My Experience

Hey there!

First of all, thanks for visiting my portfolio! You can call me Gerald, 27 year old and married (hooray!). Creating and designing websites is my passion, I am always fascinated by the facts that everything we use online on our daily lives was build by strings of letters, characters and numbers.

I love creating and designing websites!

For me, it is something that not everyone can do but once you created a website, it'll be something that everyone can find useful. For me this is the greatest revolutionary invention next to the wheel and electricity. If you find interest at my work, you can reach out to me, tell me what you envision and I will do my best to make it happen... online!

I am lucky..

to have worked at revolutionizing companies like Uber, Shopify, BigCommerce and eBay. They made the world a better place and inspire the likes of me to change the world one idea at a time. I was a team lead at Uber support team, was a Shopify Guru (yes I know ecommerce), was a tech support at BigCommerce and eBay. These experiences help me find my passion which web developing.
